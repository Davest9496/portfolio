My name is Dave, I have been writing codes for a minimum of 1 year now and loving every bit of the exciting adventure. I am based in London, United Kingdom. My interest in development started when i took courses on user interface and user experience (Ui/UX) design. I went through courses on Cousera and Udemy, towards the end of those courses we had to replicate the Figma designs we had created during the course using basic HTML, CSS and Javascript. I fell in love with coding and never looked back.

Outside of coding I do a lot of DIY, I love fixing things (even bugs). I love reading about new techs, playing chess, watching or playing football and some family time.



what are my ambitions?



Primarily, I strive to acquire a deep understanding of whatever I have chosen to do. Web/Software development fascinates me, therefore I am eager to asimilate and soak up whatever knowledge and experience that is available to me.

In the near future I want to play a part in developing and building websites and softwares with real life use case. Making life easier for the community and the world at large.



learning to code



I have used various learning materials ranging from about to video. The Havard CS50 computer science course on edx, online web development bootcamp course, the complete Javascript course, Python development, C# basics, advanced CSS and Saas all on Udemy.

I have bought and downloaded e-books on a few these programming languages, as well as very useful sites like Freecodecamp, W3schools amongs others. I have also learnt from experienced developers by reaching out to ask questions and obvserving how they organise their codes and solve problems.
